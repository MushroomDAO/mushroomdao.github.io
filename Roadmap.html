Mycelium Protocol Roadmap

A pragmatic, phased approach to building the future of travel finance, focusing on de-risking and delivering tangible value at every step.

Mycelium协议 战略路线图

我们采用务实、分阶段的方式构建旅游金融的未来，聚焦于在每个步骤中消除风险并交付可验证的价值。

Phase 0

Forging the Spear

Current - Q4 2025

Core Objective:

  • Transform the project concept into a highly executable cooperation plan that gains "principal approval" from key stakeholders.

Key Deliverables:

  • A comprehensive "Chiang Mai Pilot Compliance & Business White Paper" tailored for banks and government officials.
  • A clear "Compliance Roadmap" designed within the current legal framework.
  • A functional, simplified MVP web demo for presentation.
  • (Goal) A Letter of Intent (LOI) from CMU or a key partner showing interest in the next phase.

Absolute Priority (The ONE Thing):

  • Finalize the "Compliance & Lobbying Package." This document is the single most critical asset for convincing the first external partners.

Team & Resources:

  • The two core members lead strategy and document creation.
  • Student and part-time members support research and MVP prototype development.
阶段 0

铸矛

当前 - 2025年 Q4

核心目标:

  • 将项目构想转化为一份获得关键角色“原则性认可”的、高度可执行的合作方案。

关键成果:

  • 完成一份面向银行法务和政府官员的《清迈试点合规与商业白皮书》。
  • 制定一份在现有法律框架下的、清晰的《合规路径图》。
  • 一个可交互的、用于演示的MVP网页版Demo。
  • (理想目标) 获得一份来自清迈大学或关键伙伴的合作意向书(LOI)。

绝对优先级:

  • 完成“合规与游说方案包”。这份文件是说服第一个外部伙伴的最关键资产。

团队与资源:

  • 两位核心成员主导战略和白皮书撰写。
  • 学生和兼职成员支持资料搜集和MVP原型开发。
Phase 1

The Proving Ground

Q1 - Q2 2026

Core Objective:

  • To obtain irrefutable data in a controlled, "zero-risk" environment (Chiang Mai University) that proves the product is viable and the user experience is effective.

Key Deliverables:

  • A functional MVP for internal testing.
  • A closed-loop campus ecosystem with 50-100 student/faculty users and 2-3 partner merchants.
  • A comprehensive "CMU Sandbox Data Report" detailing user funnels, transaction success rates, and qualitative feedback.

Absolute Priority (The ONE Thing):

  • The CMU Sandbox Experiment & Report. This report is the sole passport to walk out of the campus and into the boardrooms of banks and government offices.

Team & Resources:

  • Core team manages the experiment and analyzes data.
  • Student members are key for recruiting testers and onboarding merchants.
阶段 1

练兵

2026年 Q1 - Q2

核心目标:

  • 在一个完全可控的“无风险”环境（清迈大学）中，用最小代价获取第一份“不可辩驳的数据”，证明产品可行且体验有效。

关键成果:

  • 一个功能性的、用于内部测试的MVP。
  • 一个包含50-100名师生用户和2-3家合作商家的封闭校园生态。
  • 一份详尽的《CMU校园沙盒实验数据报告》，包含用户漏斗、交易成功率和定性反馈。

绝对优先级:

  • 完成校园沙盒实验并产出数据报告。这份报告是走出校园、进入银行和政府会议室的唯一通行证。

团队与资源:

  • 核心团队负责实验管理和数据分析。
  • 学生成员是招募测试者和培训商家的关键执行力量。
Phase 2

Securing the Beachhead

Q3 2026 - Q2 2027

Core Objective:

  • To conquer the most critical fortress: securing a partnership agreement with a bank (e.g., SCB) and obtaining a "Regulatory Sandbox" permit.

Key Deliverables:

  • An official "Regulatory Sandbox" approval from the Bank of Thailand or relevant authorities.
  • A formal technical integration with SCB Bank.
  • The public launch of the official DApp with real asset conversion capabilities.
  • Achieve the first 1,000+ real users and 100+ partner merchants.
  • Generate the first protocol revenue, validating the 1.5% fee model.

Absolute Priority (The ONE Thing):

  • Sign a "Tripartite Cooperation Agreement" (Bank, Government Body, Mycelium) and receive the "Sandbox Permit." This is the project's D-Day.

Team & Resources:

  • Core members focus entirely on high-level negotiation and partnership management.
  • Technical team's primary role is to support the bank's due diligence and integration process.
阶段 2

抢滩

2026年 Q3 - 2027年 Q2

核心目标:

  • 攻克整个战役中最重要的碉堡：获得泰国银行（SCB）的合作协议和政府监管机构的“沙盒”许可。

关键成果:

  • 获得泰国银行或相关部门的官方“监管沙盒”许可。
  • 完成与SCB银行的正式技术对接。
  • 发布包含真实资产兑换功能的正式版DApp。
  • 获取首批1000+真实用户和100+合作商家。
  • 产生第一笔协议收入，验证1.5%手续费分成模型。

绝对优先级:

  • 签署“三方合作协议”（银行、政府机构、Mycelium）并获得“沙盒许可”。这是项目的诺曼底登陆日。

团队与资源:

  • 核心成员100%投入到高层沟通和谈判中。
  • 技术团队的主要角色是支持银行的技术尽职调查和集成工作。
Phase 3

Igniting the Network

Q3 2027 Onwards

Core Objective:

  • To validate the scalability and profitability of the business model in the Chiang Mai market, and to codify this success into a replicable playbook.

Key Deliverables:

  • Achieve stable growth and Product-Market Fit in Chiang Mai.
  • Publish the "City Launch Toolkit" V1.0, including technical, legal, and commercial templates.
  • Initiate the replication process by signing an MOU for a second pilot city (e.g., Bali, Lisbon).
  • (Post-validation) Conduct the Token Generation Event (TGE) for the $MYC governance token.

Absolute Priority (The ONE Thing):

  • Achieve positive unit economics in Chiang Mai and complete the "City Launch Toolkit." Perfect the recipe before opening the next franchise.

Team & Resources:

  • Team expansion begins here: hire the first full-time "Chiang Mai City Manager."
  • Core team shifts focus to strategy, fundraising, and global expansion.
阶段 3

燎原

2027年 Q3 及以后

核心目标:

  • 在清迈市场验证商业模式的规模化和盈利能力，并将成功经验“产品化”，形成可复制的行动手册。

关键成果:

  • 在清迈市场实现稳定增长和产品-市场契合（Product-Market Fit）。
  • 发布《城市启动工具包V1.0》，包含技术、法律和商业模板。
  • 签署第二个试点城市的合作备忘录（MOU），启动复制流程。
  • （在模式被充分验证后）进行$MYC治理代币的生成活动（TGE）。

绝对优先级:

  • 在清迈实现正向的单位经济效益，并完成“城市启动工具包”。在开下一家“分店”前，先把“配方”完善到极致。

团队与资源:

  • 团队扩张：招聘第一位全职的“清迈城市经理”。
  • 核心团队转向战略、融资和全球扩张。
