Roadmap 2026–2027

路线图 2026–2027

Three phases from genesis launch to a thriving ecosystem — building the infrastructure for the next economy.

从创世启动到生态成熟，三个阶段构建下一代经济的基础设施。

Vision / 愿景

We believe the future will be defined by Loose Communities and Extreme Individuals. Communities need onboarding + positive feedback + accountability (COS72). Individuals need time, space, and economic sustainability to release their imagination, creativity, and emotions.

我们相信，未来的组织形态是松散社区与极致个体。社区最需要：Onboarding + 正反馈激励 + Check = COS72。个体需要：时间、空间、经济可持续，释放自己的想象力、创造力和情感。

Phase 1 — Genesis Launch
第一阶段 — 创世启动
2026.03.30 · Active 2026.03.30 · 进行中

Focus: Cold Start & Core Infrastructure

重点：冷启动与核心基础设施

Cos72 Chrome Extension
User gateway — integrates payments and smart accounts in one browser extension.
用户入口，在浏览器插件中集成支付与智能账户。
SuperPaymaster
Decentralized Gas Payment Protocol. Let merchants sponsor users. No ETH required.
去中心化 Gas 支付协议。让商户为用户支付 Gas，用户无需持有 ETH。
AirAccount
Social login → smart contract account. No seed phrases, no friction.
社交登录 → 智能合约账户。无助记词，零门槛。
Sign90
Aggregate signature security — smart account core with guardian recovery and session keys.
聚合签名安全模块，智能账户核心，支持 Guardian 恢复与 Session Key。
iDoris — Community Brain
Community intelligence layer — content value circulation, attribution, and collaborative decision-making.
社区大脑——内容价值流通、归属确权与协作决策层。
MushroomDAO GToken
Cold launch of GToken (21M fixed supply). Initial governance vote goes live.
GToken 冷启动（2100 万恒定供应），首次治理投票上线。
