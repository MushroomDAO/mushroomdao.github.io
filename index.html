MushroomDAO builds open decentralized infrastructure for loose communities and extreme individuals — no private keys to lose, no gas fees to worry about. Inspired by 4.5 billion years of fungal cooperation.

MushroomDAO 为松散社区与极致个体构建开放的去中心化基础设施—— 无需保管私钥，无需担心 Gas 费用。灵感源自 45 亿年的菌丝协作。
